About me

Yu-Chan Jimmy Chan

What I’m doing 😎

Hi folks! I’m a graduate student pursuing a MS Degree in Computer Science and Public Policy (MSCAPP) at the University of Chicago.

With a focus at the intersection of software engineering and internet governance, I am studying how to create innovative and impactful software solutions that facilitate better digital infrastructure and human-computer/data interaction. My current niche is to learn knowledge from the core fields of high performance computing, including distributed system, parallel programming, cloud computing; and attain the skills to design large-scale, human-in-the-loop system applications and services for end-users.

I used to worked as a research fellow for Taiwan Digital Diplomacy Association. Here I developed my interests in information policy and decided to pursue a master program, and the rest is history!
